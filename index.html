Welcome to DaichiJLC

At DaichiJLC Japanese Language Center, we believe that language is the bridge to understanding culture, building careers, and opening doors to endless opportunities. Founded in 2012, we have grown from a small tutoring group to one of the most respected Japanese language institutes in the region.

Our mission is to provide world-class Japanese language education that empowers students to achieve their personal and professional goals. Whether you are preparing for the JLPT, planning to study in Japan, or seeking career advancement, our comprehensive curriculum and experienced instructors are here to guide you every step of the way.

Why Japanese?

Unlock Your Future

Learning Japanese opens doors to countless opportunities in education, career, and personal growth

Study in Japan

Access world-renowned Japanese universities and scholarship programs. Study engineering, arts, business, and more in one of the world's most innovative countries.

Employment Opportunities

Japan offers abundant job opportunities in technology, automotive, finance, and hospitality sectors. Bilingual professionals are highly sought after globally.

Cultural Understanding

Deepen your appreciation of Japanese culture, anime, manga, cuisine, and traditions. Experience Japan beyond the surface with true cultural fluency.

Global Communication

Japanese is spoken by over 125 million people. Connect with native speakers, build international relationships, and expand your global network.

Career Advancement

Bilingual professionals earn 15-20% more on average. Japanese language skills give you a competitive edge in the global job market.

Cognitive Benefits

Learning Japanese enhances memory, improves multitasking abilities, and boosts problem-solving skills. It's a workout for your brain!

FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our courses and services

Our course fees vary depending on the level and duration. N5 courses start at $299, N4 at $399, N3 at $499, N2 at $599, and N1 at $799. We offer flexible payment plans, early bird discounts, and group enrollment specials. Contact us for detailed pricing and current promotions.

We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate different lifestyles: Morning classes (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM), Afternoon classes (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM), Evening classes (6:00 PM - 9:00 PM), and Weekend intensive sessions. Online classes are available 24/7 through our learning platform. You can choose the schedule that best fits your routine.

Our JLPT preparation follows a structured approach: comprehensive grammar and vocabulary coverage, kanji memorization techniques, practice tests mirroring actual JLPT format, listening comprehension training, reading comprehension strategies, and mock examinations. We also provide study materials, flashcards, and access to our online practice platform.

Yes! We offer fully interactive online classes through our advanced virtual classroom platform. Features include live video sessions, real-time chat, digital whiteboards, shared documents, recorded sessions for review, and 24/7 access to learning materials. Online students receive the same quality education and certification as in-person students.

Registration is simple: Fill out the online application form or visit our center, take a free placement test to determine your level, consult with our academic advisor to choose the right course, complete payment (with flexible options available), and receive your welcome kit and class schedule. The entire process takes less than 30 minutes!

Absolutely! We understand that schedules change. Our hybrid learning model allows you to switch between online and in-person classes with advance notice. Many students choose a blended approach, attending in-person sessions when possible and joining online when needed. Flexibility is at the core of our teaching philosophy.
